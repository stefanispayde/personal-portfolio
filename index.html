TIC TAC TOE

This was the very first program I ever built completely by myself using jQuery/Javascript. I was so proud to have the pieces come together, by writing functions that handled the componets necessary for the game's functionality. With several projects now under my belt, I know that that much of this code is redundnt, and there are easier ways of writing a few of these functions. However as a fledgling developer, I was so excited just to have them work, and execute all the requirements and goals that were set! This is also why I lhave left it as is, without combing through and rewriting the original code. I like to be reminded of where I started to where I am now. Humbling and inspiring. Software used: HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Javascript

PET-FINDER

This app was originally a group project. It is a full MEAN stack utilizing API from Pet-Finder, an organization that has a database of all ghe adoptable animals in the US, and the shelters that house them. We loved the idea of being able to search by zipcode, include filters to narrow your search, and find the perfect pet for the user and their family.

SALON AMERICANA

Salon Americana is an older family owned salon that underwent a massive remodel, giving it a new look that was freah and modern. I was on the design team that took over their rebranding, and presented them not only with a new logo, but a new vibe completely. I started with a simple illustration which depicted a womans profile, hair windswept. This was the jumping off point for my logo design in which I took a simple yet clean sans-serif font, broke it apart, and tweaked it until it fit. This was one of the first paid freelance jobs I ever had was a stepping stone in learning how to work with clients, and produce quality work on a deadline.
